\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a directional light in the scene. 

A directional light simulates light coming from an infinitely distant source, such as sunlight, and has a specific color and direction.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
The data is aligned to meet GPU uniform buffer layout requirements.
