\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a perspective projection camera in a 3D graphics system. 

\doxylink{classeng_1_1PerspectiveCamera}{Perspective\+Camera} manages the position, orientation, and perspective projection parameters of a camera in 3D space. It calculates view and projection matrices to enable 3D scene rendering.
